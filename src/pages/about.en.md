## LITIS
The Laboratory of Computer Science, Information Processing and Systems (LITIS) is the research unit in the field of information science and technology in Haute-Normandie. It involves the three main higher education establishments in the region:

+ University of Rouen
+ University of Le Havre
+ National Institute of Applied Sciences (INSA)

For more information :
### <a href="https://www.litislab.fr"> www.litislab.fr </a>

## GREYC
The GREYC Laboratory carries out research activities in the field of digital sciences covering several aspects in computing including image processing, data mining, artificial intelligence, computer security, mathematical computing, automatic language processing, electronics and instrumentation.

For more information :
